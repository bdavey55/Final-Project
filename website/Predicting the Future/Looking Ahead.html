Looking Ahead

 

What do I think will happen with this subject? Nothing. It will continually be brushed under the rug and then swept up in movies and television, scaring the population. The fact of the matter is this: CULTS EXIST AND THEY ARE IN PLACES THAT WE WOULDN'T THINK TO LOOK.

As a population we must decide within ourselves when dedication and devotion becomes too much; though it is good to have fellow peers with the same beliefs and ideas, we must not stray far from the idea of individualism. After all, everybody is their own being; everybody has a mind, and body, and a heart. It is not healthy, or fair, to deny yourself of any freedom or liberation based on the morals of others, no matter how disturbing or even appealing they can be. I believe this new age of technology and exposure will bring cults to a bigger light, making some stronger while others die out. Just like everything in this country, people are changing. Information is changing and becoming more accessible. I just hope I never have to come across one of these groups further than researching.
