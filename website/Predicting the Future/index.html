Predicting the Future

 

So what lies ahead for this country and the presence of cults? Are cults growing stronger and more prominent? Will the general knowledge and opinion of the word "cult" become more distorted or more clear?

 

I wish i had an answer to these questions. After countless research and theories presented, it is still one of those ambiguous subjects that falls into the "wait and see" category. We will never know the full presence and impact of these groups in our nation until we join one ourselves, or one of them falls into severe power. Either way, I don't want to think about that horror.
