The Presence of Cults in America

The United States of America is, and has been for centuries, home to numerous religious, political, and organizational groups. With an ever-changing nation comes a dynamic population, with thousands of different beliefs and ideas. But sometimes, as history has shown, this severe diversity can be dangerous, for some of these organized groups take extreme measures to get their points and beliefs across. These exremists, otherwise known as "cults" are and always have been present in the world, and in our country, both hidden and sometimes in plain sight. This website is devoted to breaking down the word and exploring the ideas behind it.
