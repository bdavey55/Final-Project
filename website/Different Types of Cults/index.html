Different Types of Cults

 

So now that we tackled the word "cult," and what most people believe and do not believe it to be, it is necessary to analyze the different types of cults and organizational groups and what they stand for. These include violent, non-violent, religious, and political groups. Some of these groups and categories can intertwine, however, for example a political group can be violent or a religious group could be non-violent, etc. But for the most part, as far as storied cases and examples go, these are the four main categories that cults and organizational groups fall into.

 

  • Violent
  • Non-violent
  • Religious
  • Political
